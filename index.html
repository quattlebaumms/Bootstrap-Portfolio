About Me

My name is McKenzie Quattlebaum. I graduated from Appalachian State University in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Arts and Imaging Technology, and a minor in General Business. I now live in Charlotte, NC, where I work for a commercial print company as the Branded Merchandise Manager. I specialize in sourcing customing advertising products for corporations.

I decided to enroll in the coding bootcamp at UNCC so I could learn a new skill and hopefully make a career change in the new future. I would like to focus on front end development in my next career. When I am not at work or studying development, I enjoy training for aerial acrobatics. I currently perform on aerial silks, lyra, and static trapeze.
